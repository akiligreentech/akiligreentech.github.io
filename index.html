Our Services

At AkiliGreenTech, we have a passion for the environment, within an age of technology.

We provide a wide range of products and services to cater for every person...in the big city and in the rural homes.

Biogas

Our Biogas Solutions provides the cleanest, green, sustainable and most affordable, portable and simple to operate biogas system that can feed directly from any organic decaying matter and provide enough energy to cook and light a modern standard house.

Hydroponics

A subset of hydroculture -- a method of growing plants using mineral nutrient solutions, in water, without soil. Terrestrial plants may be grown with their roots in the mineral solution only, or in an inert medium, such as perlite or gravel. Most suitable for families living in towns.

Charcoal

From a menace to a green eco-friendly, smokeless cooking product, our chrcoal is burned from the destroyed and widely spread Prosopis trees which grows in the Arid and semi-arid regions of Kenya and which is so evasive hence trying to find ways to help control its growth and spread.

Solar Power

With enough sunlight, -- the conversion of sunlight into electricity, either directly using photovoltaics (PV), or indirectly using concentrated solar power, is one of the things that we pride ourselves with by providing an alternative to your daily power consumptions.

Tree Nursery

We grow a wide range of tree and fruit seedlings, at AkiliGreenTech, the future matters to us, that is why we provide a high quality of selected indegenous plants and fruit seedlings at an affordable price. From the great Nandi Flame to the Majestic Baobab, we all have them.

Evapocooler

With 85% of Rural areas being still not connected to the National Electricity Grid, most perishable goods go to waste due to lack of an affordable and easy to make/use form of refrigiration. The evapocooler addresses this issue and offer a long-term solution at a really affordable price.
